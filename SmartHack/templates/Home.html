Technology opens our mind

We are Adela, Miha and Raluca, and we form the 3 Threads team.
We are here to have fun, to learn interesting technologies and to meet
new people.
Our project focusses on retrieving new updates from the News API,
classifying the data in relevant terms for stock analyses.
We present the latest news from 10 FTSE 100 companies and we predict
the stock value by the end of the day based on the impact the news have
on a certain company.
